About

Where Clinical Medicine Meets AI Strategy

Pathque Consulting is led by Martin Burks IV, MD, MBA, FCAP — an AP/CP pathologist with fellowship training in cytopathology at the National Institutes of Health, a biomedical engineering foundation, and an MBA from the Darden School of Business.

As a member of the CAP Economic Affairs Committee, Martin works at the AMA/RUC level on AI reimbursement — shaping the policy infrastructure that determines how pathology AI gets paid for nationwide.

We don't sell AI tools. We build the governance infrastructure that allows your organization to adopt AI safely, compliantly, and strategically.
